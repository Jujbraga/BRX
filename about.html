About

Our story

To make a difference

BRX Consulting was founded in west Sweden by a senior engineering leader with over two decades of hands-on experience across the Automotive and Aerospace industries — in Sweden, Brazil, France, and across global supplier networks.

After years delivering product development projects for some of the world's leading manufacturers — from heavy duty trucks and passenger car systems to aircraft interiors — we kept seeing the same problems: talented engineers feeling unheard, and clients struggling to find consultants who truly understood their business.

So we built something different. BRX Consulting was founded with a simple mission: to make a difference — for engineers who deserve to be heard, and for clients who expect results.
