About Us

A startup company with more than 20 years of experience in the field in several different areas of Product Development

We are a new company headquartered in west of Sweden, which has born with more than 20 years of experience in the field in several different areas of Product Development within Automotive and Aerospace industries such as Heavy and Medium duty trucks, cars, cars systems (such as HVAC, Car Seats), Aircraft Interiors for European, USA and Latin America Markets.

We aim to be an attractive and flexible employer focused in long-term relationships with our employees, where they feel proud and can influence their own development, providing high quality consultancy service to our customers.

After several years in this industry in different countries, we saw in the market a huge opportunity to make a difference for several unhappy consultants and customers, that's why we started: to make a difference.

If you want to join us to be part of this difference, is tired of not being heard or fell like just an ordinary number where you are, don't hesitate to get in touch!!
